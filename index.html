About this app
How your data is stored and protected

🌐 Hosting

The app's code loads from GitHub Pages. Only the app itself lives there — no receipts, amounts, or personal data are ever uploaded to GitHub. Everything you record stays on this phone; receipt photos go only to your family's OneDrive.

📁 Folders & privacy

🏠 Shared — household receipts both of you can see.
🔒 Voytek-Private — on Voytek's OneDrive; Andrea cannot see it.
👤 Andrea-Private — on Andrea's OneDrive; Voytek cannot see it.

Receipts tagged 🔒 Personal never appear in shared summaries, and this phone's totals are never synced to the other phone.

💼 Work expenses

Receipts flagged as Work are kept out of your budgets — they're tracked separately so they're easy to find when submitting for reimbursement. Search "work" in Receipts.

🔐 Security

Unlocking uses your Samsung fingerprint — the same security as unlocking your phone. No biometric data is stored by this app; your fingerprint never leaves the device. The 4-digit PIN is a backup. The app allows a 30-second grace period when you switch apps, and locks after 2 minutes of inactivity.

🔒 Enabling your private folder

Choose the Personal allocation when saving, then pick your own OneDrive folder (not the family one) in the share menu. The PC sorts everything else automatically.
